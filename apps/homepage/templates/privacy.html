Privacy Policy

At Jopply, your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account data: your name and email when you sign up.
  • Application records: job postings, statuses, notes, and dates you enter.
  • Device & usage: browser type, IP address, pages visited (for performance and security).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our service: tracking your job applications and sending reminders.
  • To communicate with you: confirm actions, send notifications, and respond to requests.
  • To maintain security and prevent fraud.

3. Data Storage & Security

We store your data on secured servers with encrypted connections (HTTPS). We follow industry standards to protect against unauthorized access.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted external services (e.g., email provider for notifications). These providers have their own privacy policies—please review them before using our service.

5. Your Choices

  • You can update or delete your account at any time in your Account settings.
  • You may unsubscribe from non-critical emails via the link in each message.
  • Feel free to export your data before deleting your account.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. When we do, we will post the new version here with a revised “Last updated” date.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please email us at jopplyteam@gmail.com.
